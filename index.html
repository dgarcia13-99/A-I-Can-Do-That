Campaign Process

Phase 1: Submissions Open

We are currently accepting 60-second videos showcasing how you use AI to facilitate a process in your daily life. Submit your entry to be considered!

Phase 2: Expert Review

Our panel of judges will review all submissions and select the top 5 most innovative and impactful AI implementations.

Phase 3: Employee Voting

The final 5 submissions will be showcased for company-wide voting to determine the winner.

How to Submit Your Story

  1. Record a 60-second video showcasing how you use AI in your work
  2. Keep it simple and focus on the practical benefits
  3. Show real examples of your AI-powered workflow
  4. Highlight the impact on your productivity
  5. Share any tips or insights for colleagues who might want to try similar approaches
